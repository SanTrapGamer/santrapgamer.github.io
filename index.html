Arnold Palmer: The Greatest Golfer of All Time

Arnold Palmer c.1958

Arnold Palmer is known as one of the greatest golfers of all time. He has managed to claim numerous victories over his time on the tour. Below is a list of information that relates to his accomplishments and life.

  • 1959: September 20 in the city of Latrobe, Pennsylvania, Arnold Palmer was born.
  • 1954: Arnold Palmer joins the PGA Tour as a golf professional.
  • 1955: Arnold Palmer wins his first PGA Tournament: The Canadian Open.
  • 1958: Arnold Palmer wins his first Masters Tournament.
  • 1980: Arnold Palmer joins the PGA Tour of Champions during its first official year.
  • 2006: Arnold Palmer retires as a professional golfer.
  • 2016: September 25 in the city of Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, Arnold Palmer died.
